# Decoctions
## Base Recipes and Dosage
**Strong**
- 1 oz herbs
- 2 pints water
- simmer 10 mins
- steep 15 mins

**Moderate**
- 2/3 oz herbs
- 2 pints water
- simmer 10 mins
- steep 15 mins

**Weak (Tea)**
- 1/3 oz herbs
- 2 pints water
- simmer 8 mins
- steep 15 mins

_From: The Master Book of Herbalism by Paul Beyerl_
